About Us

Our story, our values, our promise to you

Est. 2019

We Believe Everyone Deserves Fresh, Affordable Food

Paygreens Supermarket was founded in 2019 with a simple mission: bring the freshest, highest-quality groceries to every household — at prices that make sense. What started as a small neighbourhood store in Abuja has grown into a beloved online destination trusted by over 50,000 families across Nigeria.

We partner directly with local farmers, cutting out the middleman so you get produce that's truly farm-to-table fresh, while farmers get fair prices for their hard work. It's a win-win that we're proud to champion every single day.

Our team of 200+ passionate people work around the clock to pack, store, and deliver your groceries with the care they deserve. From our temperature-controlled warehouses to our friendly delivery riders, every step is designed with your satisfaction in mind.
